AUD appreciation (which has been a feature of 2025-26 given USD weakness) creates a mechanical headwind to AUD-denominated EPS and DPS even if underlying USD earnings grow.

5. **Regulatory/compliance risk**: Gambling regulation is tightening across multiple jurisdictions (Australia's online gambling reforms, UK white paper implementation, US state-level changes).
